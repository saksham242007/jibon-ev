Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Jibon, our platform, and how we’re shaping a smarter way to move in cities.

What is Jibon and what problem does it solve? +
Jibon is a mobility-first platform designed to simplify everyday commuting. We focus on reducing friction in urban movement by combining smart technology with user-friendly experiences.
Do I need the app to use Jibon services? +
Yes. Our website is built for discovery and information. All core actions such as booking, managing rides, and tracking usage happen seamlessly inside the Jibon mobile app.
Is Jibon available in all cities? +
We are expanding step by step. Availability depends on city readiness, infrastructure, and partnerships. Updates are always announced in the app.
How is Jibon different from other mobility platforms? +
Jibon is built with an app-first mindset, clean design philosophy, and long-term sustainability goals. We prioritize reliability, simplicity, and user trust over complexity.
Can businesses collaborate with Jibon? +
Absolutely. We work with logistics partners, enterprises, and local teams to create tailored mobility solutions. Reach out via our contact page.
What’s next for Jibon? +
We’re focused on scaling responsibly, improving user experience, and continuously innovating to make daily movement easier and smarter.
