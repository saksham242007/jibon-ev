Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about advertising with Jibon Electric and reaching customers through our electric vehicle network.

How does advertising on Jibon electric vehicles work?

Jibon Electric allows brands to place advertisements on our electric scooters and three-wheelers that operate across the city daily. As these vehicles travel through busy areas, your brand gains continuous visibility and reaches thousands of people throughout the day.

What types of businesses can advertise with Jibon Electric?

Any business can advertise with Jibon Electric, including startups, local shops, restaurants, e-commerce brands, educational institutes, and large companies. Our platform helps businesses increase brand awareness and reach more customers.

What are the benefits of advertising on electric scooters?

Scooter advertising provides high visibility in busy city areas and reaches different neighborhoods throughout the day. It is also a cost-effective and eco-friendly way for brands to promote their products and services.

How long can I run an advertising campaign?

Advertising campaigns can run for flexible durations such as one month, three months, six months, or longer depending on your marketing goals. Businesses can choose a campaign plan that best fits their promotion strategy.

How can I start advertising with Jibon Electric?

To start advertising, simply contact our team through the website or WhatsApp and share your campaign details. Our team will guide you through the process and help launch your advertising campaign quickly.
