OUR MISSION

At Jibon Electric, our mission is to make electric mobility accessible and affordable for every rider by replacing fuel-based transport with scalable EV solutions. We are solving India’s last-mile delivery challenges through clean, efficient, and income-generating mobility systems.

OUR VISION

We envision a future where every delivery and commute is powered by sustainable electric mobility. Jibon Electric aims to build India’s largest EV rental ecosystem, empowering millions of riders while accelerating the transition toward a greener and scalable transportation network.

Jibon Electric Growth Roadmap

Our long-term vision is to build the largest electric delivery fleet across Northeast India before expanding nationwide with our own EV vehicle portfolio.

2026

Launch & Assam Expansion

Launch our pilot fleet with 30 electric scooters in Guwahati,Assam. During the same year we will scale rapidly across Assam and aim to deploy 1,000+ scooters through logistics and delivery partnerships.

2027

Regional Expansion & Vehicle Diversification

Expand operations across Northeast states including Meghalaya, Tripura and other nearby cities. Fleet size will grow to 2,000+ scooters. While introducing our first electric three-wheeler fleet. During this phase we will also begin developing our own electric scooter brand and start working on protype design and engineering.

2028

Product Launch

Launch our first Jibon-branded delivery scooter designed specifically for last-mile logistics operations. The Fleet will scale to 5,000+ scooters and 1,000+ electric three-wheelers across Northeast India.

2029

Nationwide Expansion

Enter national expansion phase with operations across multiple Indian cities. Fleet scale will exceed 10,000+ scooters and 3,000+ three-wheelers. Launch multiple Jibon EV models for different logistics and commercial mobility use cases.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Jibon Electric, our services, and franchise opportunities.

What is Jibon Electric and what does the company do?

Jibon Electric is an electric mobility company focused on 2-wheeler and 3-wheeler EV rentals, fleet solutions, advertising, and franchise opportunities across India.

Who can become a Jibon Electric franchise partner?

Entrepreneurs, fleet operators, delivery partners, and investors looking to enter the EV mobility space can apply for a Jibon Electric franchise.

How does Jibon Electric support delivery riders?

We provide electric scooters on rent, helping delivery partners reduce fuli costs and increase daily profits. Our vehicles are built for reliability, low maintenance, and consistent performance.

Is Jibon Electric suitable for delivery and gig workers?

Absolutely. Our EVs are designed for daily delivery operations with low running costs, high reliability, and minimal maintenance.

How can I apply or get more information?

You can apply directly through our website or contact our team for detailed guidance on investment, setup, and operations.
