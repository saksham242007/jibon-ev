About Jibon Electric

Jibon Electric is redefining mobility by creating a scalable electric vehicle ecosystem that empowers both riders and businesses. From electric two-wheelers to cargo three-wheelers and mobile advertising, we enable efficient and sustainable transportation solutions.Starting from the North East India, we are building a strong foundation focused on affordability, reliability, and accessibility. Our platform helps riders earn, businesses grow, and cities move toward a cleaner future.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Jibon Electric, our services, and franchise opportunities.

What is Jibon Electric and what does the company do?

Jibon Electric is an electric mobility company focused on 2-wheeler and 3-wheeler EV rentals, fleet solutions, advertising, and franchise opportunities across India.

Who can become a Jibon Electric franchise partner?

Entrepreneurs, fleet operators, delivery partners, and investors looking to enter the EV mobility space can apply for a Jibon Electric franchise.

Does Jibon Electric provide vehicles and operational support?

Yes. We provide EV vehicles, fleet management support, branding, technology, and operational guidance to help partners scale efficiently.

Is Jibon Electric suitable for delivery and gig workers?

Absolutely. Our EVs are designed for daily delivery operations with low running costs, high reliability, and minimal maintenance.

How can I apply or get more information?

You can apply directly through our website or contact our team for detailed guidance on investment, setup, and operations.
