Jibon Electric Growth Roadmap

Our long-term vision is to build the largest electric delivery fleet across Northeast India before expanding nationwide with our own EV vehicle portfolio.

2026

Launch & Assam Expansion

Launch our pilot fleet with 30 electric scooters in Guwahati,Assam. During the same year we will scale rapidly across Assam and aim to deploy 1,000+ scooters through logistics and delivery partnerships.

2027

Regional Expansion & Vehicle Diversification

Expand operations across Northeast states including Meghalaya, Tripura and other nearby cities. Fleet size will grow to 2,000+ scooters. While introducing our first electric three-wheeler fleet. During this phase we will also begin developing our own electric scooter brand and start working on protype design and engineering.

2028

Product Launch

Launch our first Jibon-branded delivery scooter designed specifically for last-mile logistics operations. The Fleet will scale to 5,000+ scooters and 1,000+ electric three-wheelers across Northeast India.

2029

Nationwide Expansion

Enter national expansion phase with operations across multiple Indian cities. Fleet scale will exceed 10,000+ scooters and 3,000+ three-wheelers. Launch multiple Jibon EV models for different logistics and commercial mobility use cases.

Frequently Asked Questions

Discover how you can partner with Jibon Electric and grow with our expanding EV fleet network.

What is the Jibon Electric franchise?

The Jibon Electric franchise is an opportunity where partners invest in electric scooters and become part of our growing fleet network. Jibon Electric manages the operations, deployment, and maintenance of the vehicles.

Do investors need to operate the scooters themselves?

No. Investors are not required to manage or operate the scooters. Jibon Electric handles fleet management, rider allocation, maintenance, and daily operations.

What is the minimum investment required?

The minimum investment starts from 10 scooters. Based on the current cost of ₹60,000 per scooter, the total investment for 10 scooters is approximately ₹6,00,000.

What returns can investors expect?

Investors can expect an estimated 36% return on investment (ROI) over 3 years, depending on fleet performance and operational efficiency.

How can I start investing with Jibon Electric?

To start investing, simply select the number of scooters using our investment calculator and submit your details. Our team will contact you and guide you through the investment process.
